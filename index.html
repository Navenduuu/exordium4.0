About

Exordium 4.0

EXORDIUM 4.0, the fourth edition of the annual flagship event of the IEEE Student Branch GEC Barton Hill,Thiruvananthapuram is a three-day offline event that brings together students from GEC Barton Hill and several other colleges. The event features a vibrant mix of technical workshops and enriching activities aimed at enhancing technical, managerial, and collaborative skills.

about

IEEE SB GECBH

Government Engineering College Barton Hill since its inception in January 2005 has shown valor in every stage of its existence, one better than the next. IEEE Student Branch of Government Engineering College Barton Hill is the spin-off of integrated efforts by a diligent and lively crew. The zealousness with which our members engage in various events is stupendous. We have unfailingly generated prominent professionals, widely appreciated and known for their contributions to various tech spheres. Our Student branch has come a long way off and we are certain to accomplish many more milestones ahead.

Here, at IEEE SB GECBH we have a pulsating and vibrant group of young professionals to boast of, who are constantly striving towards transformation and upheaval in the field of technology. We are consistently working to unveil innovative events and workshops to benefit all, to strengthen the technical, interpersonal and networking skills of all involved. Moreover, we try our utmost to ensure that our work benefits the society, holding close to heart one of the main ideals fostered by IEEE. To be a part of this dynamic community will truly prove an enlightening experience to every technophile out there.

Why Exordium

EXORDIUM 4.0 aims to mould young professionals with industrial expertise and skills essential for technological advancement benefiting humanity. Tune in and enjoy the extravaganza of a Technical Fest.

Prominent Speakers

Renowned experts and industry leaders will share their knowledge, insights, and experiences with participants. Their sessions will provide valuable perspectives on emerging technologies, leadership, and professional growth.

Innovative Minds

The event will bring together creative thinkers and problem-solvers from diverse domains. Participants will collaborate, compete, and showcase groundbreaking ideas that inspire innovation and future possibilities.

Networking & Mentorship

Students will connect with industry leaders, IEEE professionals, and peers from across institutions. Such interactions open doors for mentorship, career guidance, and future collaborations.

Innovation & Creativity

The platform will encourage participants to think critically and propose novel solutions during events. This nurtures an innovative mindset that is essential for entrepreneurship and research.

Technical Expertise

Participants will gain hands-on exposure to cutting-edge tools and technologies through diverse workshops and competitions. This will help them apply theoretical knowledge to practical problem-solving in real-world contexts.

Leadership & Collaboration Opportunities

By engaging in gallery-based activities, students will sharpen their leadership, teamwork, and decision-making skills. These experiences foster confidence to take on roles in academic, professional, and community projects.
